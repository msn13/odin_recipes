Avocado-Mango Salsa

Fresh Fruit and Vegetable Salsa

This salsa is quick and easy to make, plus it's delicious! Once you taste it, you won't be able to stop eating it!

Ingredients

  • 1 mango - peeled, seeded and diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, seeded and diced
  • Â¾ cup chopped red onion
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and diced
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Toss together mango, bell pepper, onion, sugar, olive oil, and vinegar in a bowl. Gently fold in diced avocado, and season with salt.
